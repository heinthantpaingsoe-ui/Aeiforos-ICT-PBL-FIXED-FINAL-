Yangon: 2026

The Breaking Points

Yangon is facing big problems as the city grows. Power cuts, traffic jams, and poor waste management make daily life harder. Air pollution from cars and factories makes the city less healthy to live in.

1

Power Outages

Yangon faces frequent blackouts because of weak power infrastructure and high electricity demand. Many areas experience daily outages and rely on diesel generators, while renewable energy use remains very low.

Daily outage duration (peak): 8hrs

Households with backup power: 35%
Renewable energy share: 4%

Grid reliability index: 22%

2

Traffic Congestion

Yangon faces heavy traffic congestion because of rapid urban growth, limited public transport, and increasing private vehicle use. Long travel times and air pollution are common during peak hours.

Average traffic delay: 62mins

Public transport usage: 28%

Daily commuters affected: 1.9M

3

Waste Management

Yangon struggles with waste management because of increasing garbage production and limited recycling systems. Some areas face illegal dumping, overflowing bins, and pollution in drains and rivers.

Waste collected daily: 2,500 tons

Recycling rate: 5%

Population with regular collection: 68%

4

Carbon Emissions

Yangon has high carbon emissions because of traffic, factories, and fossil fuel electricity.

Air pollution rate: 78%

Renewable energy usage: 4%

Main pollution source: Unsustainable Transportation
