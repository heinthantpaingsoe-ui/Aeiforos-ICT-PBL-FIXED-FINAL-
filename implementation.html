Our Plan

Implementing the features from other sustainable smart cities to make Yangon a more sustainable and smart city.

Waste Management

Adapted from: Zurich

Yangon should improve its urban waste management system by increasing recycling programs and using smarter waste collection methods. This can reduce landfill waste, improve cleanliness, and create a healthier urban environment.

Expected Impact:

Reduce landfill waste by 30% within 5 years

Main Steps

  • Introduce separate recycling bins in urban areas
  • Improve waste collection schedules using smart monitoring systems
  • Build more recycling and waste processing facilities
  • Educate the public about proper waste disposal
  • Reduce plastic waste through environmental policies

Solar Farms & Distributed energy

Adapted from: Singapore

To increase the use of renewable energy, Yangon can invest in solar farms and distributed energy systems. This involves installing solar panels on rooftops, public buildings, and open spaces to generate clean electricity. Additionally, implementing microgrids can help manage energy distribution more efficiently and reduce reliance on fossil fuels.

Expected Impact:

Reduce power outages durations by 60% within 3 years

Main Steps

  • Conduct a feasibility study for solar farm implementation
  • Secure funding and partnerships for the project
  • Design and install solar panels on suitable rooftops and public buildings
  • Implement distributed energy systems and microgrids
  • Monitor and evaluate the performance of the solar farms and energy systems

Carbon Emissions

Adapted from: Zurich

The city should reduce carbon emissions by using cleaner energy sources and promoting sustainable urban transport. This helps improve air quality and supports a healthier environment for residents.

Expected Impact:

Reduce urban carbon emissions by 20% within 5 years

Main Steps

  • Increase the use of renewable energy sources
  • Encourage the use of electric public transport
  • Plant more trees and expand green urban spaces
  • Improve energy efficiency in buildings
  • Monitor emission levels using smart environmental systems

Traffic Congestion

Adapted from: Singapore

Yangon should improve urban traffic flow by developing smarter transport systems and better road management. This can reduce travel time, improve urban mobility, and lower pollution caused by traffic congestion.

Expected Impact:

Reduce traffic congestion by 40% within 5 years

Main Steps

  • Introduce smart traffic light systems
  • Expand public transport services in urban areas
  • Use GPS tracking for buses and traffic monitoring
  • Create dedicated lanes for buses and emergency vehicles
  • Improve road planning and traffic management systems
