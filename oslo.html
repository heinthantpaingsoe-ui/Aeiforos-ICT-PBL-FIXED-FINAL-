Oslo

A leading sustainable city famous for renewable energy, electric transportation, and green urban planning.

Oslo is the capital city of Norway and is known for its renewable energy systems, green transportation, and environmentally friendly urban planning. It is one of the world’s leading sustainable cities and strongly focuses on reducing pollution and carbon emissions.
