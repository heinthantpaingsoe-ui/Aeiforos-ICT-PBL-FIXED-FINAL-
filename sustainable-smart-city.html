Sustainable Smart Cities

Sustainable Smart Cities are types of cities that utilize new and eco-friendly technology, whilst putting the enviorment first in its priotity. It is a mix modern technology and natures beauty.

Singapore

Singapore is a highly developed city-state known for it's smart technology, clean environment, and efficient urban planning. It is one of the leading smart and sustainable cities in the world, it is located in Southeast Asia, with a population of 5.6 million people. Singapore is known for being a clean city with advanced technology and strong government planning, and it has a nickname called "Smart Nation".

Sergio Sala

Zurich

Zurich is Switzerland's largest city and a global financial hub, blending medieval charm with modern, cosmopolitan urban life. Located on Lake Zurich with Limmat River views, it offers high-end shopping, museums, and over 1,200 drinking fountains. Known for extreme cleanliness and efficiency, it acts as a cultural and economic powerhouse.

Henrique Ferreira

Oslo

Oslo is the capital and largest city of Norway, located at the head of the Oslo Fjord. It is a vibrant hub known for blending modern, sustainable architecture with easy access to nature. Key attractions include the Munch Museum, Vigeland Sculpture Park, and the opera house, plus it is home to the Nobel Peace Prize ceremonyn by getting called "Sustainability of Northern Europe ".

Martian Rotea

Copenhagen

Copenhagen is the capital and largest city of Denmark, known as a vibrant, eco-friendly, and highly livable coastal city located on the islands of Zealand and Amager. It blends historic royal charm with modern design, world-class dining, and a strong bicycle culture, making it a premier European destination focused on sustainability.

Lindsay Martin
